<p><p>Electromagnetic flow meters are utilized for measuring the flow rate of electrically conductive liquids like water and chemicals. There are two main types of electromagnetic flow meters - Direct-Current Type and Induction Type. The Direct-Current Type utilizes a magnetic field generated by direct current to measure the flow rate, while the Induction Type uses alternating current to induce a voltage in the fluid and measure the flow rate. Both types offer accurate and reliable flow measurements for various industries.</p></p>
