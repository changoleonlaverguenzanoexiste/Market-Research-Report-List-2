<p><strong>The Asphalt Release Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid</li><li>Solid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Asphalt release agents are available in two main types: liquid and solid. Liquid release agents are typically sprayed onto the surface of the mold or formwork before pouring asphalt to prevent it from sticking. They are easy to apply and provide a smooth finish. Solid release agents come in the form of wax or powder and are applied by rubbing onto the mold. They create a barrier between the mold and the asphalt, making it easier to remove the finished product. Both types are commonly used in the construction industry to improve efficiency and quality of asphalt applications.</p></p>
