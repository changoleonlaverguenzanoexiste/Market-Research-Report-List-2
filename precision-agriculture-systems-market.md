<p><p>Precision Agriculture Systems are categorized into three main market types - guidance system, remote sensing, and variable-rate technology. Guidance systems help farmers navigate and operate agricultural equipment with precision. Remote sensing technologies collect data from fields using drones or satellites to monitor crop health and yield potential. Variable-rate technology automates the application of inputs such as water, fertilizer, and pesticides based on real-time data, optimizing resources and maximizing yields. These market types collectively contribute to maximizing efficiency and sustainability in agriculture.</p></p>
