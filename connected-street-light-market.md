<p><ul><li>Wired (Power Line Communication)</li><li>Wireless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Connected street lights can be classified into two main market types: wired (Power Line Communication) and wireless. Wired street lights use existing power lines to enable communication between the lights and a central control system. This allows for more stable and reliable connectivity but may require significant infrastructure upgrades. On the other hand, wireless street lights utilize radio frequency technology to transmit data between the lights and the control system. This type of connectivity is more flexible and easier to deploy, but may be less reliable in certain environments.</p></p>
