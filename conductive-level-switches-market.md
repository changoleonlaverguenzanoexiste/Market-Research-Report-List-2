<p><p>Conductive level switches are available in two main types - Integral Type and Split Type. Integral Type switches consist of a single unit where the sensing element and control unit are integrated into one device. Split Type switches, on the other hand, are separated into two units - the sensing element and the control unit. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different industries and environments based on their specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
