<p><p>Medical catheter coating market types include hydrophilic coating, anticoagulant coating, antimicrobial coating, and other coatings. Hydrophilic coatings are designed to reduce friction and enhance lubricity, making catheter insertion smoother. Anticoagulant coatings help prevent blood clotting and improve device performance. Antimicrobial coatings inhibit the growth of bacteria and reduce the risk of infections. Other coatings may include drug-eluting coatings or specialized materials for specific patient needs. Each type of coating serves a specific purpose in improving the functionality and safety of medical catheters.</p></p>
