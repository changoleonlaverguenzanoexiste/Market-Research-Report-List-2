<p><p>Variable Air Volume (VAV) Box Market comprises different types based on their functionality and design. Single-Duct Variable Air Volume systems use a single duct to supply air to different zones. Dual-Duct Variable Air Volume systems use two separate ducts for hot and cold air, allowing for precise temperature control. Fan-Powered Variable Air Volume systems use fans to regulate air flow, providing better control over air distribution. Other types of VAV Boxes include pressure-dependent and pressure-independent systems, offering unique features for specific building requirements.</p></p>
