<p><p>Meat Protein Analyzer market is divided into two types based on technology - DEXA Technology and NIR Technology. DEXA Technology, which stands for Dual Energy X-ray Absorptiometry, uses X-ray technology to measure the composition of meat samples quickly and accurately. On the other hand, NIR Technology, which stands for Near-Infrared Spectroscopy, utilizes light absorption to analyze the protein content in meat samples. Both technologies offer efficient and reliable methods for assessing protein levels in meat products.</p></p>
