<p><p>The Automotive Adjustable Steering System market is segmented into two main types: Manually Adjustable Steering and Electrically Adjustable Steering. Manually Adjustable Steering systems require physical adjustment by the driver through a lever or knob, providing a more hands-on approach to customization. On the other hand, Electrically Adjustable Steering systems use electronic controls to allow for easier and more precise adjustments, often providing additional features such as memory settings. Both types offer drivers the ability to tailor their steering preferences for a more comfortable and personalized driving experience.</p></p>
