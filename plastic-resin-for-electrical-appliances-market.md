<p><p>Plastic resin for electrical appliances market is divided into various types such as PP, PS, ABS, SAN, and others. PP (polypropylene) is known for its flexibility and resistance to heat and chemicals. PS (polystyrene) offers clarity and insulation properties. ABS (acrylonitrile butadiene styrene) is strong and durable. SAN (styrene-acrylonitrile) combines the properties of styrene and acrylonitrile. Other types of resins offer their own unique characteristics and benefits for use in electrical appliances. These different types cater to diverse needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
