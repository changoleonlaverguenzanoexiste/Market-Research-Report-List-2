<p><ul><li>Adsorption Control Agent</li><li>Oxidation Control Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Odour control agents are used to eliminate or reduce unpleasant odours in various industries. Adsorption control agents work by trapping odour molecules on their surface, effectively removing the odour. Oxidation control agents, on the other hand, work by chemically reacting with the odour molecules to break them down into non-odorous compounds. Both types of odour control agents play a crucial role in maintaining a pleasant environment in facilities such as waste treatment plants, manufacturing facilities, and agricultural operations.</p></p>
