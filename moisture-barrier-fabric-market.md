<p><p>Breathable Moisture Barrier Fabric allows air and moisture vapor to pass through while blocking liquids and stains, making it ideal for use in products like breathable waterproof clothing and mattress protectors. On the other hand, Non-Breathable Moisture Barrier Fabric completely blocks moisture and liquids from passing through, making it suitable for use in medical supplies and industrial applications where protection from spills and leaks is paramount. Both types of fabrics provide effective moisture barrier protection, but cater to different needs and preferences.</p></p>
