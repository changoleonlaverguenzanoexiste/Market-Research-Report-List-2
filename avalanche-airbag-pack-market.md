<p><strong>The Avalanche Airbag Pack Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Canister Based Airbag</li><li>Fan Based Airbag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Canister Based Airbag Packs use a pressurized gas canister to deploy the airbag in the event of an avalanche, providing increased protection and buoyancy to the user. This type of airbag is reliable and effective in quickly inflating the airbag. On the other hand, Fan Based Airbag Packs use a battery-powered fan to inflate the airbag, offering a lighter and more compact design. While they may be slower to deploy compared to canister-based airbags, they are reusable and easier to travel with.</p></p>
