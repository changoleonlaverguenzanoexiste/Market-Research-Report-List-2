Free lutein refers to the unbound form of lutein, while lutein esters are lutein molecules bonded with fatty acids. Both forms have similar benefits for eye health, but lutein esters are believed to have greater stability and bioavailability. Consumers can choose between these two forms based on their preferences and specific health needs.</p></p>
