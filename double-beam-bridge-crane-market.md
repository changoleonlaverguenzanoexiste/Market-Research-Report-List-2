<p><p>Double beam bridge crane market includes two main types: Gantry crane and Half-Gantry crane. Gantry crane is a type of crane that is supported by legs on both sides of the span, typically used in outdoor applications. Half-Gantry crane, on the other hand, is similar to a gantry crane but only has one leg supported by the structure, making it suitable for indoor use where space is limited. Both types of cranes are commonly used in industries such as construction, manufacturing, and logistics for material handling and lifting operations.</p></p>
