Dial indicators have a circular dial with a pointer that gives a visual representation of the level, while digital indicators provide a numerical display of the level. Both types of indicators are commonly used in various industries such as manufacturing, food and beverage, and chemical processing to ensure accurate monitoring of levels for operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
