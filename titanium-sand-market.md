<p><p>Titanium sand market is categorized into four main types based on mineral composition: rutile, ilmenite, leucoxene, and others. Rutile is known for its high titanium dioxide content and is considered the most valuable type. Ilmenite is the most abundant form and is commonly used in pigment production. Leucoxene is a weathered form of ilmenite with higher titanium dioxide content. Other types may include blends or mixed compositions of the three main forms. Each type has varying uses and market demand.</p></p>
