<p><p>Gas detection systems are crucial for ensuring safety in various industries. Different types of gas detection systems are available in the market, including Semiconductor Type, Electrochemical, Catalytic Combustion, Photoionization (PID), and Infrared Sensor systems. Semiconductor Type systems use a semi-conductor sensor to detect gases, while Electrochemical systems rely on chemical reactions. Catalytic Combustion systems use a catalyst to detect gases, PID systems use ultraviolet light to ionize gases, and Infrared Sensor systems detect gases by measuring their absorption of infrared radiation.</p></p>
