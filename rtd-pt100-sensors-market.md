<p><p>RTD (PT100) sensors are widely used in various industries to measure temperature accurately. There are two main types of RTD sensors: Wire-Wound RTD and Thin Film RTD. Wire-Wound RTDs are made by winding a platinum wire around a ceramic or glass core, providing high accuracy and stability. On the other hand, Thin Film RTDs are created by depositing a thin film of platinum on a ceramic substrate, offering faster response times and better long-term stability. Both types play a crucial role in temperature measurement applications.</p></p>
