<p><p>The specialty barley malt market consists of various types of malt, including Roasted Malt, Crystal Malt, and Dark Malt. Roasted Malt is made by roasting the barley at high temperatures, resulting in a rich, toasty flavor. Crystal Malt is created by kilning the barley at different temperatures to produce a range of flavors from sweet to caramel-like. Dark Malt is manufactured through prolonged kilning or roasting, developing deep, intense flavors like chocolate or coffee. Each type of malt offers unique characteristics for brewers and distillers to enhance their products.</p></p>
