<p><p>Podded thrusters are categorized into two main market types based on motor placement: built-in motor and external motor. Built-in motor podded thrusters have the motor integrated into the pod housing, offering a more compact design and easier installation. On the other hand, external motor podded thrusters have the motor located outside the pod housing, providing easier access for maintenance and repairs. Each market type has its own advantages and considerations, catering to different requirements and preferences in the marine industry.</p></p>
