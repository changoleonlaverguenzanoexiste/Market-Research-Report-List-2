<p><p>Particle board furniture is available in various types in the market. Laminated particle board provides a decorative finish with a protective layer. Veneered particle board has a thin layer of real wood on the surface for a natural look. Cement bonded particle board is made by bonding wood particles with cement for added strength and durability. Melamine particle board has a decorative melamine finish that is resistant to scratches and heat. These different types cater to various preferences and offer a range of options for consumers in the furniture market.</p></p>
