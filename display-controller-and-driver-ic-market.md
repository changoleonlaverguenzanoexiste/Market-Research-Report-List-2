<p><p>A Display Controller is a device designed to manage the display of information on a screen or monitor. It acts as an interface between the microprocessor and the display hardware, translating digital data into analog signals that can be understood by the display. A Driver IC, on the other hand, is responsible for controlling the individual pixels on a display, enabling the display to show images or videos.</p><p>The Display Controller and Driver IC Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for displays in various applications such as smartphones, televisions, automotive displays, and wearable devices. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.1% during the forecast period.
