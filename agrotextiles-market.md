<p><p>Agrotextiles are categorized into different types based on their manufacturing process and material composition. The main types of agrotextiles are Woven, Knitted, Nonwoven, and Others. Woven agrotextiles are made by weaving together yarns to create a strong and durable fabric. Knitted agrotextiles are created by interlocking loops of yarn to form a flexible and stretchable material. Nonwoven agrotextiles are produced by bonding fibers together without weaving or knitting. The "Others" category includes unique and specialized agrotextiles that may not fit into the other three main types.</p></p>
