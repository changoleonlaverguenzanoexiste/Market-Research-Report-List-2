<p><strong>The PVDC Barrier Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PVDC Resins</li><li>PVDC Latex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVDC Barrier Material Market includes PVDC Resins and PVDC Latex. PVDC Resins are solid resins used for extrusion coating and laminating applications, providing excellent barrier properties to extend the shelf life of packaged goods. PVDC Latex, on the other hand, is a water-based dispersion used for coating flexible packaging materials, offering similar barrier benefits as PVDC Resins. Both types of PVDC barriers are essential for industries such as food packaging, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics to protect products from moisture, oxygen, and other external factors.</p></p>
