<p><p>Biostimulants are substances that enhance plant growth and improve nutrient uptake. There are two main types in the agriculture market: acid-based biostimulants and extract-based biostimulants. Acid-based biostimulants contain organic acids that help improve soil structure and increase nutrient availability. Extract-based biostimulants are derived from natural sources such as seaweed or plant extracts, which contain beneficial compounds like amino acids and hormones that stimulate plant growth. Both types of biostimulants are used to improve crop yield and quality in sustainable farming practices.</p></p>
