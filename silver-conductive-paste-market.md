<p><strong>The Silver Conductive Paste Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Side Silver Paste</li><li>Back Side Silver Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Front side silver paste is used in the manufacturing of solar cells and semiconductor devices to create electrical connections on the front side of the cell or device. This paste is designed to provide high conductivity and good adhesion to the substrate. Back side silver paste, on the other hand, is used on the back side of solar cells to enhance the overall efficiency by reflecting light back into the cell. Both types of silver paste play crucial roles in the production of high-quality electronic and photovoltaic devices.</p></p>
