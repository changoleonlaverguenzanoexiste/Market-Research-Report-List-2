<p><p>Pharmaceutical grade macroporous resin materials are classified based on their polarity levels into nonpolarity, medium polarity, and polarity markets. Nonpolarity resins have low affinity for polar substances and are ideal for separating nonpolar compounds. Medium polarity resins have moderate affinity for both nonpolar and polar substances, making them versatile for a wide range of applications. Polarity resins have high affinity for polar substances, allowing for efficient separation of complex mixtures with diverse polarities. Each type of resin offers unique benefits for specific purification processes in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
