<p><ul><li>LargeFlatbed Scanner</li><li>Feed-through scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wide format scanners are categorized into two main types: large flatbed scanners and feed-through scanners.</p><p>Large flatbed scanners are ideal for scanning delicate, oversized, or irregularly shaped documents and objects. They have a flat surface where the document is placed for scanning, allowing for high-quality, detailed scans of large items such as maps, blueprints, or artwork.</p><p>Feed-through scanners, on the other hand, are designed for scanning long documents or continuous rolls of paper. They are commonly used in industries like architecture, engineering, and construction for scanning large volumes of documents quickly and efficiently.</p></p>
