<p><strong>The Automotive TPMS（Tire-Pressure Monitoring System) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct TPMS</li><li>Indirect TPMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive TPMS (Tire-Pressure Monitoring System) market includes two types: Direct TPMS and Indirect TPMS. Direct TPMS uses sensors within the tire to directly measure air pressure, providing precise and real-time data. Indirect TPMS utilizes the vehicle's anti-lock braking system to monitor individual wheel speeds and detect differences that indicate low tire pressure. Both systems aim to improve safety by alerting drivers of underinflated tires, reducing the risk of accidents and improving fuel efficiency.</p></p>
