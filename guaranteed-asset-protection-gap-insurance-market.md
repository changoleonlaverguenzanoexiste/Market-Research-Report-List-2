<p><p>Guaranteed Asset Protection (GAP) Insurance is available in different market types to suit varying needs. Finance GAP Insurance covers the shortfall between the insurance payout and the outstanding finance amount. Return-to-invoice GAP Insurance reimburses the original purchase price of the vehicle. Vehicle Replacement GAP Insurance covers the cost of replacing the vehicle with a new one. Return-to-value GAP Insurance pays the difference between the current value of the vehicle and the original purchase price. Other market types may offer additional coverage options tailored to specific situations.</p></p>
