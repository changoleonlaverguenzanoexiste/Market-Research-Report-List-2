<p><strong>The Pneumatic Piston Vibrator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impacting Piston Vibrators</li><li>Non-Impacting Piston Vibrators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pneumatic Piston Vibrators are classified into two main types based on their operation: Impacting Piston Vibrators and Non-Impacting Piston Vibrators. Impacting Piston Vibrators produce vibrations through the impact of a piston hitting the base, creating high-amplitude vibrations perfect for moving heavy materials. Non-Impacting Piston Vibrators, on the other hand, generate vibrations through a continuous piston motion without impact, ideal for applications requiring smoother and gentler vibrations. Both types offer distinct advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
