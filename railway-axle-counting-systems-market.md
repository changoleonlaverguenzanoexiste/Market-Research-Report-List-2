<p><p>Railway Axle Counting Systems Market consists of two main types: Rail Side Installation and On-rail Installation. Rail Side Installation involves mounting the counting system on the side of the rail tracks, enabling easy access for maintenance and monitoring. On the other hand, On-rail Installation involves placing the system directly on the railway tracks, providing a more accurate and efficient counting process. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of railway operators, offering versatility and flexibility in monitoring axle counts.</p></p>
