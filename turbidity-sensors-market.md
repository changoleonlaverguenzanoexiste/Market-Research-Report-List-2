<p><strong>The Turbidity Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Turbidity Sensor</li><li>Digital Turbidity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Analog turbidity sensors measure the amount of light scattered or absorbed by particles in a liquid to determine turbidity levels. These sensors provide continuous, real-time data on water quality. Digital turbidity sensors, on the other hand, convert the turbidity reading into digital data for easier processing and analysis. Both types of sensors are widely used in water treatment plants, breweries, and research laboratories to monitor and control turbidity levels for various applications.</p></p>
