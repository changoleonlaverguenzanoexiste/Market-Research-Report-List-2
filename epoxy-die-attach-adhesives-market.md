<p><strong>The Epoxy Die Attach Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrically Insulative</li><li>Electrically Conductive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Epoxy die attach adhesives are used in the semiconductor industry for bonding chips to substrates. There are two main types in the market: electrically insulative and electrically conductive. Electrically insulative adhesives ensure that there is no electrical conduction between the chip and substrate, preventing short circuits. On the other hand, electrically conductive adhesives allow for the flow of electricity between the chip and substrate, providing a reliable electrical connection. Both types play a crucial role in the performance and functionality of electronic devices.</p></p>
