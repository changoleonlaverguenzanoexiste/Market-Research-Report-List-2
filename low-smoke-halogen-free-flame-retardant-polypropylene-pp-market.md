<p><p>Low Smoke Halogen Free Flame Retardant Polypropylene (PP) can be classified into three types based on their fire performance according to IEC standards - IEC60332-1, IEC60332-2, and IEC60332-3. IEC60332-1 refers to cables that prevent vertical flame spread, IEC60332-2 refers to cables that prevent the spread of fire in a bundle, and IEC60332-3 refers to cables that limit the propagation of fire along a cable. These standards ensure that PP materials used in various applications meet specific fire safety requirements.</p></p>
