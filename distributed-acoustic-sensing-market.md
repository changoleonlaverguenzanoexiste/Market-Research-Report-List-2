<p><strong>The Distributed Acoustic Sensing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing</li><li>Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) technology is utilized in two main market types: Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing and Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing. Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing involves detecting and measuring acoustic signals that travel in the same direction as the wave's propagation, providing valuable information for applications such as seismic monitoring and pipeline leak detection. Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing, on the other hand, focuses on detecting acoustic signals that travel perpendicular to the wave's propagation, suitable for applications in structural health monitoring and perimeter security.</p></p>
