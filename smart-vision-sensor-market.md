<p><p>Smart vision sensors are advanced imaging devices that utilize artificial intelligence and machine learning to process visual information in real-time. The market for smart vision sensors can be categorized into two main types: 3D vision sensing and traditional 2D vision sensing. 3D vision sensing allows for depth perception and spatial awareness, enabling more complex applications such as robotic navigation and object recognition. On the other hand, traditional 2D vision sensing is mainly used for simple object detection and tracking. Both types of smart vision sensors are essential in various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, healthcare, and security.</p></p>
