<p><ul><li>Cold Applied</li><li>Hot Applied</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bituminous adhesive market consists of two main types: Cold Applied and Hot Applied. Cold Applied bituminous adhesives are typically used for bonding materials at room temperature without the need for heating. They are convenient and easy to apply, making them ideal for small scale projects. On the other hand, Hot Applied bituminous adhesives require heating before application and are commonly used for large scale projects where a stronger bond is needed. Both types are widely used in construction and roofing industries for various applications.</p></p>
