<p><p>Neutral density filters are categorized into two main types in the market - non-reflective type and reflective type. Non-reflective type filters are designed to reduce the amount of light passing through without affecting color balance, making them ideal for photography and videography. Reflective type filters, on the other hand, are used to reduce glare and reflections, making them suitable for applications where minimizing unwanted light reflections is important. Both types of filters serve different purposes and cater to specific needs in the photography and videography industry.</p></p>
