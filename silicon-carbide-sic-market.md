<p><p>Silicon Carbide (SIC) is classified into two types based on color: Black SiC and Green SiC. Black SiC is used mainly in the manufacturing of abrasive tools such as grinding wheels, cutting disks, and sandpapers due to its high hardness and ability to withstand high temperatures. On the other hand, Green SiC is preferred for applications requiring higher purity and thermal conductivity, such as making ceramic materials and semiconductor devices. Both types have their unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
