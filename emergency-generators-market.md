<p><p>Emergency generators market can be divided into two main types: portable generators and standby generators. </p><p>Portable generators are typically smaller, mobile units that can be easily transported to different locations and are usually powered by gasoline or propane. They are ideal for powering small appliances or tools during power outages or outdoor activities.</p><p>Standby generators, on the other hand, are larger, permanent fixtures that are connected to a building's electrical system. They automatically turn on when a power outage is detected and are able to power an entire household or building for an extended period of time.</p></p>
