There are three main types of Heat Resisting Alloys: Iron Base Superalloy, Nickel Base Superalloy, and Cobalt Base Superalloy. Iron Base Superalloys contain iron as the primary alloying element, while Nickel Base Superalloys have nickel as the main component.
