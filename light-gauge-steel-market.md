<p><p>Light Gauge Steel Market can be categorized into different types such as C Type, T Type, U Type, and Others. C Type is a channel-shaped steel used primarily in framing applications. T Type refers to a T-shaped steel used for structural framing. U Type is a U-shaped steel commonly used for roof and floor joists. The category of Others includes various other types of light gauge steel products used in construction, such as Z-shaped steel and omega-shaped steel. Each type serves specific purposes in the construction industry.</p></p>
