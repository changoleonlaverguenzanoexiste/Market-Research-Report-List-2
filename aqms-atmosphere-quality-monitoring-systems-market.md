<p><p>AQMS market is divided into two main types: Portable Monitoring Systems and Stationary Monitoring Systems. Stationary systems are larger, fixed installations placed in permanent locations to continuously monitor air quality over a longer period of time. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and real-time monitoring of atmospheric quality for environmental and health purposes.</p></p>
