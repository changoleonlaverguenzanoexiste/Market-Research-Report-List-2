<p><ul><li>Optical Absorption Spectrometer</li><li>Optical Emission Spectrometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Desktop spectrometers can be broadly categorized into two types based on their operating principles: Optical Absorption Spectrometer and Optical Emission Spectrometer. Optical Absorption Spectrometers analyze the absorption of light by a sample to determine its chemical composition, while Optical Emission Spectrometers detect and analyze the emission of light from a sample when it is excited by a high-energy source. Both types of spectrometers are commonly used in research, quality control, and material analysis applications.</p></p>
