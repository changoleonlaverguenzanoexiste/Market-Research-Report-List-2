<p><strong>The Aerosol Insecticides Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Foam Aerosols</li><li>Powder Aerosols</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aerosol insecticides are available in various types, including foam aerosols and powder aerosols. Foam aerosols are designed to quickly expand upon application, covering a larger surface area for more efficient pest control. On the other hand, powder aerosols are finely powdered insecticides that are dispersed in the air to reach hidden pests in cracks and crevices. Both types offer convenient and effective solutions for dealing with insect infestations in homes, offices, and other indoor spaces.</p></p>
