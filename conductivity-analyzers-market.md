<p><p>Conductivity analyzers are used to measure the ability of a solution to conduct electricity. There are three main types of conductivity analyzers in the market - Contacting, Electrodeless, and Others. Contacting analyzers use probes with direct contact with the solution being tested. Electrodeless analyzers use coils to induce a current in the solution without direct contact. Other analyzers may use different technologies such as toroidal sensors. Each type of analyzer has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
