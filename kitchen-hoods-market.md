<p><strong>The Kitchen Hoods Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Detached Type</li><li>Embedded Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kitchen hoods are available in two main types in the market - detached type and embedded type. Detached type hoods are usually mounted on the wall above the cooking area and are separate from the cooktop. They are often more affordable and easier to install. On the other hand, embedded type hoods are built into the cabinetry above the cooktop, offering a more seamless and integrated look in the kitchen. They are typically more expensive and require professional installation.</p></p>
