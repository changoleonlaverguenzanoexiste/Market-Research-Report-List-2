<p><p>UD Carbon Fiber Prepreg is a composite material that combines unidirectional carbon fiber with a matrix material, either thermoset or thermoplastic, to create a strong and lightweight product. Thermoset prepreg materials use a resin that undergoes a chemical reaction when cured, resulting in a rigid and durable material. In contrast, thermoplastic prepreg materials soften when heated and harden when cooled, allowing for reshaping and recycling. Both types of prepreg offer unique advantages and are used in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and sports equipment.</p></p>
