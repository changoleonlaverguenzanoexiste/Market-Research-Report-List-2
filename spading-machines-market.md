<p><ul><li>PTO-driven</li><li>Walk-behind</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spading machines come in various types, including PTO-driven and walk-behind models. PTO-driven spading machines are powered by a tractor's power take-off mechanism, making them suitable for larger scale operations and heavy-duty tasks. Walk-behind spading machines, on the other hand, are designed for smaller scale gardening or landscaping projects, providing more control and maneuverability for operators. Both types of machines offer efficient soil preparation by breaking up and turning over the soil, making them essential tools for agricultural and gardening applications.</p></p>
