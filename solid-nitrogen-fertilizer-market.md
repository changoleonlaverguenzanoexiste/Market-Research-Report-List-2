<p><p>Solid nitrogen fertilizer market types include urea, ammonium nitrate, ammonium sulfate, calcium ammonium nitrate (CAN), and others. Urea is a widely used nitrogen fertilizer known for its high nitrogen content. Ammonium nitrate is another popular choice due to its fast-acting properties. Ammonium sulfate is favored for its sulfur content, which is essential for plant growth. CAN is a mixture of calcium carbonate and ammonium nitrate, providing a balanced supply of nutrients. Other nitrogen fertilizers may include blends or specialty products tailored for specific crops or soil conditions.</p></p>
