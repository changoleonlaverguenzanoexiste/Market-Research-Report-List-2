<p><p>Automotive starters are essential components in starting the engine of a vehicle. The electric starter is the most common type, utilizing an electric motor to crank the engine. Pneumatic starters use compressed air to start the engine, commonly found in diesel engines. Hydraulic starters function similarly to pneumatic starters but use hydraulic fluid instead of air. Each type has its benefits and applications based on the specific needs of the vehicle, such as power output, efficiency, and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
