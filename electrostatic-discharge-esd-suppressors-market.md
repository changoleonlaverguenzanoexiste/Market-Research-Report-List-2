<p><p>Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) Suppressors are used to protect electronic devices from damage caused by electrostatic discharge. There are three main types of ESD suppressors on the market: Diode Arrays, Polymer, and TVS. Diode Arrays are integrated circuits that provide ESD protection by shunting excess energy away from sensitive components. Polymer suppressors are made from conductive polymers that absorb and dissipate ESD energy. TVS (Transient Voltage Suppression) suppressors are designed to limit voltage spikes and prevent damage to electronic equipment.</p></p>
