<p><p>A Rotor Stator Pump is a type of positive displacement pump that operates using a helical rotor turning within a fixed stator. This pumping mechanism creates a pulsating flow, making it ideal for applications such as transferring thick, viscous fluids or handling abrasive materials. Rotor Stator Pumps are commonly used in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, wastewater treatment, and oil and gas.</p><p>The Rotor Stator Pump Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 15% during the forecast period.
