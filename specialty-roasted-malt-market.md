<p><p>Specialty roasted malt market can be divided into three types based on the type of grain sourced - barley sourced, wheat sourced, and rye sourced. Barley sourced roasted malts are the most common and versatile option, providing a rich, malty flavor. Wheat sourced roasted malts offer a lighter, crisp flavor profile, ideal for lighter beer styles. Rye sourced roasted malts bring a spicy and earthy character to the beer, adding complexity and depth to the final product. Each type of malt offers brewers a unique flavor profile to experiment with.</p></p>
