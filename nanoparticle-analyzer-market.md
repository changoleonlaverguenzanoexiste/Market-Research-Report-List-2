<p><p>Nanoparticle analyzers are utilized to measure various characteristics of nanoparticles in different markets. Size analyzers determine the size distribution of nanoparticles, while zeta potential analyzers measure the surface charge to evaluate stability. Weight analyzers measure the mass of nanoparticles, and flow properties analyzers examine their movement within a liquid. Each market type offers specific benefits for analyzing nanoparticles in different industries, such as healthcare, cosmetics, and environmental science, providing valuable insights for research and development purposes.</p></p>
