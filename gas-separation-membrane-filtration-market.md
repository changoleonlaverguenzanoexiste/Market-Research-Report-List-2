<p><p>Gas separation membrane filtration market types include hollow fiber, spiral wound, and others. Hollow fiber membranes consist of thousands of small tubes for gas separation, providing high surface area for filtration. Spiral wound membranes are constructed with a flat sheet membrane and feed spacer wound around a permeate tube, allowing for efficient separation. Other market types may include tubular, capillary, or ceramic membranes. Each type offers unique advantages in gas separation applications, catering to different industries and requirements.</p></p>
