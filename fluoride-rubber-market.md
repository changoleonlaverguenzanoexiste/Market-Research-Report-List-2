<p><p>Fluoride rubber is a type of elastomer material that is resistant to heat, chemicals, and oils. There are three main types of fluoride rubber in the market: Fluorocarbon Elastomers, Fluorosilicone Elastomers, and Perfluoroelastomers. Fluorocarbon Elastomers are known for their excellent resistance to fuels and oils, while Fluorosilicone Elastomers offer high temperature stability and low compression set. Perfluoroelastomers are the most chemically resistant and can withstand extreme temperatures, making them ideal for harsh environments such as those in the aerospace and semiconductor industries.</p></p>
