<p><p>CO2 sampling lines are categorized into two main types based on flow rate: Low-flow nasal sampling lines and High-flow nasal sampling lines. Low-flow nasal sampling lines are designed for use with low-flow oxygen therapy systems, typically delivering oxygen at a rate of 1-6 liters per minute. On the other hand, high-flow nasal sampling lines are specifically designed for use with high-flow oxygen therapy systems, delivering oxygen at rates greater than 6 liters per minute. These different types cater to varying patient needs and oxygen flow rates.</p></p>
