<p><p>VOC treatment and recovery market includes various technologies such as regenerative thermal oxidation, recuperative thermal oxidation, catalytic oxidation, photo-oxidation, adsorption by activated carbon, rotor concentrators, cryocondensation, and other emerging technologies. Regenerative thermal oxidation involves high-temperature combustion to break down VOCs. Recuperative thermal oxidation uses a heat exchanger to improve energy efficiency. Catalytic oxidation utilizes catalysts to lower the temperature needed for VOC combustion. Photo-oxidation involves using UV light to oxidize VOCs. Adsorption by activated carbon captures VOCs on a porous surface. Rotor concentrators and cryocondensation concentrate VOCs for treatment. Other emerging technologies are also being developed to improve VOC treatment efficiency.</p></p>
