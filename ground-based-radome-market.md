<p><ul><li>Shell Structure</li><li>Spherical Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ground-Based Radome Market includes two main types of structures: Shell Structure and Spherical Structure. Shell Structure radomes are typically made of flat panels assembled in a dome shape, providing flexibility in design and construction. On the other hand, Spherical Structure radomes are constructed by wrapping a continuous fabric around a frame to form a rounded shape. Both types provide protection for radar equipment from harsh weather conditions while ensuring signal transmission and reception accuracy. The choice between the two depends on specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
