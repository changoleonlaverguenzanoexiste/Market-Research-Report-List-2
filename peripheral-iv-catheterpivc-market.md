<p><p>Peripheral IV Catheter (PIVC) market consists of two main types: Short PIVC and Integrated/Closed PIVC. Short PIVCs are commonly used for short-term infusions or medication administration, typically ranging from 1-3 days. Integrated/Closed PIVCs have components such as needleless connectors and extension tubing integrated into the catheter hub, reducing the risk of contamination and bloodstream infections. They are often used for longer durations, up to 14 days. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of healthcare providers and patients.</p></p>
