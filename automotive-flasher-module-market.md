<p><p>The Automotive Flasher Module market is segmented into two main types: Electronic Flasher and Thermal Flasher. Electronic Flasher modules use electronic circuits to control the blinking frequency of turn signals, whereas Thermal Flasher modules utilize a bi-metallic strip that heats up to control the timing of the blinking. Electronic Flashers offer more precise control and are becoming more popular due to their reliability and durability, while Thermal Flashers are simpler in design and are often found in older vehicles or for cost-effective solutions.</p></p>
