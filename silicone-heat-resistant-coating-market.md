<p><p>Silicone heat resistant coatings are commonly categorized into three main types based on their application in the coating system. The primer is the initial layer applied to the substrate to enhance adhesion and corrosion resistance. The topcoat is the final layer that provides protection against weathering and UV exposure. The middle layer serves as a barrier to heat transfer and enhances overall thermal insulation properties. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring the durability and performance of the coating system in various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
