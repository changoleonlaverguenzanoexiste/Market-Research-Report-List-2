<p><p>Fluoro Synthetic Rubber market consists of three main types: Fluorocarbon Rubber, Fluorosilicone Rubber, and Fluorinated Phosphonitrile Rubber. Fluorocarbon Rubber offers excellent resistance to heat, oil, and harsh chemicals, making it ideal for various industrial applications. Fluorosilicone Rubber, a combination of silicone and fluorine, provides superior resistance to fuels and hydraulic fluids. Fluorinated Phosphonitrile Rubber boasts exceptional resistance to high temperatures and chemicals. Each type of fluoro synthetic rubber caters to specific needs in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
