<p><strong>The Electric Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact-type</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric testers are used to check the presence of electricity in a circuit. Contact-type testers require physical contact with the wire or circuit to measure voltage, while contactless testers can detect voltage without direct contact. Contact-type testers are commonly used for simple household tasks, while contactless testers are preferred for working with live wires or in potentially dangerous situations. Both types are important tools for electricians and DIY enthusiasts to ensure the safety and functionality of electrical systems.</p></p>
