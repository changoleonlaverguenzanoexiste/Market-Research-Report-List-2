In blown film applications, EBA provides high impact resistance and toughness. In tie-layer applications, it improves adhesion between dissimilar materials. In coextrusions, EBA acts as a barrier against moisture and gases. In blending, EBA enhances flexibility and durability. In extrusion coating, it provides excellent adhesion and sealing properties, making it suitable for food packaging and industrial coatings.</p></p>
