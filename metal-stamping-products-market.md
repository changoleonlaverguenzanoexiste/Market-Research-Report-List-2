<p><p>Metal stamping products market includes various types such as blanking process, embossing process, bending process, coining process, and flanging process. Blankling process involves cutting out a piece of metal, embossing process creates raised designs on the metal surface, bending process involves shaping the metal by bending it, coining process produces raised designs with great detail, and flanging process involves bending the edge of metal to create a flange. Each process serves a specific purpose in the metal stamping industry.</p></p>
