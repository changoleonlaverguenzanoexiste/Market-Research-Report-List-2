<p><p>Bio-Aviation Fuel market types include FT SPK, HEFA SPK, SIP, FT-SPK/A, ATJ-SPK, and Others. FT SPK refers to synthetic paraffinic kerosene produced through Fischer-Tropsch process. HEFA SPK is hydroprocessed esters and fatty acids made from vegetable oils. SIP stands for synthetic isoparaffins. FT-SPK/A is a combination of FT SPK and aromatics. ATJ-SPK is alcohol-to-jet synthetic paraffinic kerosene. Others include bio-aviation fuels not falling into the mentioned categories. Each type offers different compositions and production methods for sustainable aviation fuel.</p></p>
