<p><p>Backup generators are essential for providing power during outages. There are two main types in the market: portable generators and standby generators. Portable generators are typically smaller and can be moved easily, making them ideal for powering specific appliances or tools during emergencies. Standby generators, on the other hand, are larger and permanently installed to provide backup power to an entire home or business. Both types serve the purpose of ensuring continuous power supply during outages, but cater to different needs and requirements.</p></p>
