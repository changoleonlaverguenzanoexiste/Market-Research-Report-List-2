<p><p>The visual processor market can be divided into two main types: Independent GPU and Integrated GPU. Independent GPUs are separate graphics processing units that are standalone components and offer high performance for demanding graphics applications such as gaming and professional graphic design. Integrated GPUs, on the other hand, are built into the central processing unit and offer lower performance but are more cost-effective and energy-efficient. Both types cater to different market segments based on the required level of graphics performance and budget considerations.</p></p>
