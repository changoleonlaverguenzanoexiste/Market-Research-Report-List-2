<p><p>Radio Frequency cables are categorized into two main types based on impedance: 50Ω and 75Ω. The 50Ω cables are commonly used in industrial and military applications for their high power handling capacity, making them ideal for transmitting signals over long distances with minimal loss. On the other hand, the 75Ω cables are more suitable for consumer electronics and broadcasting applications due to their lower impedance and higher bandwidth capabilities, making them perfect for transmitting high-quality audio and video signals with maximum fidelity.</p></p>
