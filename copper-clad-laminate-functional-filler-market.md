<p><p>Copper Clad Laminate Functional Fillers are materials added to copper clad laminates to enhance their properties. Types of functional fillers include Silica Fume, Aluminum Hydroxide, Titania, Aluminum Nitride, Hexagonal Boron Nitride, Silicon Carbide, and others. These fillers improve thermal conductivity, mechanical strength, and insulation properties of the laminates. Silica Fume enhances electrical insulation, Aluminum Hydroxide improves flame retardancy, Titania enhances mechanical strength, Aluminum Nitride and Hexagonal Boron Nitride improve thermal conductivity, and Silicon Carbide provides high thermal conductivity and mechanical strength.</p></p>
