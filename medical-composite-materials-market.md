<p><p>Medical composite materials market includes various types such as fiber composites, polymer-metal composites, and polymer-ceramic composites. Fiber composites consist of a matrix material reinforced with fibers to enhance strength and stiffness. Polymer-metal composites combine the properties of both polymer and metal materials, offering lightweight and corrosion-resistant solutions. Polymer-ceramic composites combine the flexibility of polymers with the hardness and heat resistance of ceramics. Each type of composite material offers unique characteristics and benefits that cater to different applications in the medical industry.</p></p>
