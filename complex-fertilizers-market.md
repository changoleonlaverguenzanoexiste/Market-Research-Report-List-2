<p><strong>The Complex Fertilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incomplete Complex Fertilizers</li><li>Complete Complex Fertilizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Complex fertilizers are categorized into two main types: Incomplete and Complete complex fertilizers. Incomplete complex fertilizers contain only one or two primary nutrients such as nitrogen, phosphorus, or potassium. On the other hand, Complete complex fertilizers contain a combination of all essential primary nutrients along with secondary and micronutrients. Both types of complex fertilizers are essential for promoting plant growth and increasing crop yields, with Complete complex fertilizers providing a wider range of nutrients for balanced plant nutrition.</p></p>
