Tissue Forceps are used for grasping and manipulating tissues during surgery, while Atraumatic Vascular Clamps are used to occlude blood vessels without causing damage. Buffer Vein Retractors help in exposing the surgical field, and Bulldog Clamps are used for temporary occlusion of blood vessels. Other instruments in the market cater to specific needs and requirements of kidney transplant surgeries.</p></p>
