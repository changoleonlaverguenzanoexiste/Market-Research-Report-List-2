<p><p>Transformer Rectifier Unit (TRU) market consists of different types based on control mechanisms- Automatic, Manual, and CVCC Controlled. Automatic TRU systems regulate output voltage and current automatically without manual intervention, making it convenient for users. Manual TRU systems require manual adjustment for regulating output, giving users more control. CVCC Controlled TRU systems use Capacitor Voltage Clamping Circuit technology for efficient regulation of output voltage and current. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to diverse needs in industries such as aviation, power generation, and manufacturing.</p></p>
