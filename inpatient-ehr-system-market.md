<p><p>Inpatient EHR systems are available in two main market types - cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers, accessible through the internet, and are known for their flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premises systems are installed locally on a healthcare facility's own servers and are managed in-house. They offer more control over data security and customization but can be more expensive and require dedicated IT resources for maintenance and updates.</p></p>
