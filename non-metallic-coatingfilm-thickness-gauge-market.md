<p><p>Non-metallic Coatingfilm Thickness Gauge market includes two main types: Wet Film Side Thickness Gauge and Dry Film Side Thickness Gauge. Wet Film Side Thickness Gauge measures the thickness of wet coatings before they are dried, allowing for adjustments to the application process. Dry Film Side Thickness Gauge, on the other hand, measures the thickness of dried coatings, ensuring that the final product meets required specifications. Both types of gauges play a crucial role in ensuring accuracy and consistency in coating applications across various industries.</p></p>
