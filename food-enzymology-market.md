<p><p>Food Enzymology Market Types include Beta-Glucanase, Xylanase, Pectinase, Cellulase, A-Amylase, and Neutral Protease. Beta-Glucanase is used to break down beta-glucans in grains and improve dough quality. Xylanase helps in breaking down xylan in plant cell walls, improving dough elasticity. Pectinase breaks down pectin in fruits and vegetables, aiding in juice extraction. Cellulase breaks down cellulose in plant cell walls. A-Amylase breaks down starch into simple sugars. Neutral Protease helps in protein hydrolysis, improving texture and flavor in food products.</p></p>
