<p><p>Blood separation equipment market includes various types such as plasmapheresis machines, centrifuges, blood pumps, and others. Plasmapheresis machines are used to separate plasma from blood cells, while centrifuges are used to separate components of blood based on density. Blood pumps are used to transfer blood between different components of the separation process. Other types of equipment in the market may include filtration systems or automated systems for blood separation. These devices are crucial in medical facilities for procedures like blood transfusions and plasma donations.</p></p>
