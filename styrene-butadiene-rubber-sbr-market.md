<p><p>Styrene-butadiene rubber (SBR) is available in two main types in the market: Emulsion Type and Solution Type. Emulsion Type SBR is produced by emulsion polymerization, allowing for easy blending with other materials and creating products with good durability and resilience. On the other hand, Solution Type SBR is produced through solution polymerization, resulting in a higher molecular weight and superior performance characteristics. Both types are widely used in various industries such as automotive, construction, and footwear for their excellent properties and versatility.</p></p>
