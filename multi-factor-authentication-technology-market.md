<p><p>Multi-factor authentication technology market offers different types based on the number of factors involved in the authentication process. Two-factor authentication requires two authentication factors like a password and a pin. Three-factor authentication adds another factor like a fingerprint scan. Four-factor authentication includes factors like facial recognition or voice recognition. Five-factor authentication involves additional factors like security questions or authentication tokens. These different types cater to varying levels of security needs and provide options for organizations to choose the right level of protection for their data.</p></p>
