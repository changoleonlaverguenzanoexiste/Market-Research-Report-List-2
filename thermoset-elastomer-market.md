<p><p>Thermoset elastomers are a type of rubber that, once molded, cannot be remelted or reshaped. Common types include Nitrile Rubber (NBR), known for its oil and fuel resistance; Ethylene Propylene (EPR), valued for its weatherability and electrical insulation properties; and Fluorocarbon Elastomers (FKM), recognized for its chemical resistance and high temperature tolerance. Other types of thermoset elastomers include Silicone Rubber, Polyurethane, and Styrene Butadiene Rubber (SBR), each offering unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
