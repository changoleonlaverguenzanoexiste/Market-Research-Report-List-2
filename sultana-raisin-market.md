<p><p>Natural dry sultanas are dried in the sun without the use of any artificial heat sources, resulting in a darker color and a more intense flavor. They are considered more traditional and have a higher demand in the market for their natural process. In contrast, artificial dry sultanas are dried using artificial heat sources such as ovens, which speeds up the drying process and results in a lighter color and a milder flavor. They are popular for their uniform appearance and consistent quality.</p></p>
