<p><strong>The Lignin Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Kraft Lignin</li><li>Lignosulphonates</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lignin products market can be categorised into three main types - Kraft Lignin, Lignosulphonates and others. Kraft Lignin is produced primarily through the kraft pulping process and is used in various applications such as dispersants, binders and stabilizers. Lignosulphonates are derived from the sulfite pulping process and find applications in industries such as construction, animal feed and agriculture. The "Others" category includes various forms of lignin products such as organosolv lignin and soda lignin, each with their own unique properties and applications.</p></p>
