<p><p>Rare Earths are categorized into different market types based on their specific applications and properties. Cerium is used in catalysts, polishing compounds, and glass production. Magnet rare earths include neodymium, which is essential for producing powerful magnets. Dysprosium oxide is utilized in nuclear reactors and motors. Other rare earth markets include europium for color displays and terbium for electronic devices. Each type of rare earth plays a crucial role in various industries.</p></p>
