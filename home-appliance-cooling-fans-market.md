<p><ul><li>Axial Fans</li><li>Centrifugal Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Home appliance cooling fans typically fall into two main market types: Axial Fans and Centrifugal Fans. Axial fans are designed to move air parallel to the fan's axis, making them more suitable for applications requiring high airflow at low pressures. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, are designed to move air perpendicular to the fan's axis, making them more suitable for applications requiring higher pressure and air delivery. Both types of fans play a crucial role in maintaining optimal temperatures and preventing overheating in various home appliances.</p></p>
