<p><p>Bone tumor ablation devices market includes various types such as Radiofrequency Ablation, Microwave Ablation, Cryoablation, and Others. Radiofrequency ablation involves using high-frequency electrical currents to heat and destroy tumor cells. Microwave ablation utilizes electromagnetic waves to heat and kill the tumor tissue. Cryoablation involves freezing the tumor using extremely cold temperatures. Other types of ablation devices may include laser ablation or ultrasound-guided ablation. These advanced technologies provide minimally invasive and effective treatment options for patients with bone tumors.</p></p>
