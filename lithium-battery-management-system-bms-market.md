<p><p>The Lithium Battery Management System (BMS) market can be categorized into two main types: Centralized BMS and Distributed BMS. </p><p>Centralized BMS involves a single control unit managing the entire battery pack, providing centralized monitoring and control. On the other hand, Distributed BMS consists of multiple control units distributed throughout the battery pack, allowing for more flexibility and scalability. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on factors such as application requirements, cost considerations, and system complexity.</p></p>
