<p><strong>The Strip Rolling Mills Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-High Rolling Mills</li><li>Four-High Rolling Mills</li><li>Six-High Rolling Mills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Strip rolling mills market can be classified into three types based on the number of rolls involved in the rolling process. Two-high rolling mills have two rolls for symmetric rolling, while four-high rolling mills have four rolls for more precise and uniform thickness of the strip. Six-high rolling mills are equipped with six rolls for better control and flexibility in the rolling process. Each type of rolling mill offers unique advantages and is used in different industries based on specific requirements.</p></p>
