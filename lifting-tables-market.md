<p><p>Lifting tables are available in three main types: electric, hydraulic, and pneumatic. Electric lifting tables use an electric motor to raise or lower the platform. Hydraulic lifting tables use hydraulic fluid and a pump to lift heavy loads. Pneumatic lifting tables use compressed air to raise the platform. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for specific applications. Electric tables are often preferred for their ease of use, while hydraulic tables are known for their strength and lifting capacity. Pneumatic tables are commonly used in clean environments due to their lack of oil.</p></p>
