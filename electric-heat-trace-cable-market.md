<p><p>Electric heat trace cables are used for a variety of applications such as pipe freeze protection, roof and gutter de-icing, and temperature maintenance in industrial processes. The market is divided into two main types: self-regulating heaters and constant wattage heaters. Self-regulating heaters automatically adjust their heat output based on the surrounding temperature, making them more energy-efficient and easier to control. Constant wattage heaters provide a consistent level of heat output regardless of temperature fluctuations, making them ideal for applications where a steady heat source is required.</p></p>
