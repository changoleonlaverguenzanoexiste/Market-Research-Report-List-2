<p><p>Bamboo Decking comes in various types including Solid Bamboo Decking, which is made from solid pieces of bamboo; Engineered Bamboo Decking, which is made from layers of bamboo glued together for increased durability; and Strand Woven Bamboo Decking, which is made from shredded bamboo fibers compressed together. Other types of bamboo decking may include Bamboo Composite Decking, which combines bamboo fibers with other materials for added strength, and Bamboo Plywood Decking, which is made from thin layers of bamboo glued together into a sheet. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of durability, sustainability, and aesthetics.</p></p>
