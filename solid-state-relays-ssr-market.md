<p><p>Solid State Relays (SSRs) are electronic switches that use semiconductor devices to control the flow of current. They are available in three main market types: PCB Mount, Panel Mount, and Din Rail Mount. PCB Mount SSRs are designed to be soldered directly onto circuit boards for compact application. Panel Mount SSRs are installed on control panels for easy access and operation. Din Rail Mount SSRs are specifically designed for mounting on standard din rails in industrial settings for efficient installation and maintenance.</p></p>
