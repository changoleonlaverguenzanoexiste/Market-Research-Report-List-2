Electric hoists are powered by electricity, providing more consistent and efficient lifting for moderate loads. Air hoists use compressed air for operation, offering a clean and efficient option for heavy lifting. Hydraulic hoists use hydraulic fluid for power, making them ideal for heavy loads and frequent use due to their durability and strength.</p></p>
