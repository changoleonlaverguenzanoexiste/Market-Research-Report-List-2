<p><p>One-Component Mortars are ready-to-use concrete repair products that only require the addition of water before application. These are convenient and easy to use but may have shorter shelf lives. Two-Component Mortars require the mixing of two separate components before application, offering better performance and durability compared to one-component mortars. While they may be more labor-intensive, two-component mortars are often preferred for repair projects that require higher strength and performance levels.</p></p>
