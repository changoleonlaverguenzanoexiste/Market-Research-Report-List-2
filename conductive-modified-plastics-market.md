<p><strong>The Conductive Modified Plastics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structural Conductive Plastic</li><li>Composite Conductive plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conductive modified plastics are categorized into two main types: structural conductive plastic and composite conductive plastic. </p><p>Structural conductive plastic refers to materials that are inherently conductive and can be used as structural components in electronic devices. On the other hand, composite conductive plastic consists of a non-conductive matrix filled with conductive materials, providing both mechanical strength and electrical conductivity. Both types are widely used in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and aerospace for their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
