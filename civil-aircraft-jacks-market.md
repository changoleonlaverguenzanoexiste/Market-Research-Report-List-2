<p><ul><li>Tripod Jack</li><li>Axle Jack</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tripod jacks and axle jacks are two commonly used types of jacks in the civil aircraft industry. Tripod jacks are used for lifting the aircraft from the fuselage area, providing stability and support. Axle jacks, on the other hand, are used for lifting the aircraft by the landing gear axle, allowing for maintenance and repairs to be conducted on the wheels and landing gear system. Both types of jacks play crucial roles in the maintenance and servicing of civil aircraft, ensuring safety and efficiency in aviation operations.</p></p>
