<p><p>Magnesium Oxide, also known as magnesia, is a mineral compound with various market types. Dead-Burned Magnesium Oxide (DBM) is produced by calcining natural magnesite at high temperatures, while Fused Magnesium Oxide (FM) is manufactured by melting magnesite in an electric arc furnace. Caustic Calcined Magnesium Oxide (CCM) is obtained by heating magnesite with caustic soda. Synthetic Magnesium Oxide is produced through chemical processes. Each type of magnesium oxide serves different industrial purposes based on their specific properties and production methods.</p></p>
