<p><strong>The IGZO Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amorphous IGZO</li><li>Crystalline IGZO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IGZO displays are classified into two main types: amorphous IGZO and crystalline IGZO. Amorphous IGZO displays are made of randomly arranged atoms, resulting in a disordered structure that allows for faster electron mobility and better transparency. Crystalline IGZO displays, on the other hand, have atoms arranged in a highly ordered structure, leading to higher electron mobility and improved stability. Both types of IGZO displays offer high resolution, low power consumption, and improved performance for various electronic devices.</p></p>
