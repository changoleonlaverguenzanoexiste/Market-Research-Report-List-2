<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RFID Transponders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive RFID Transponders</li><li>Active RFID Transponders</li><li>Semi-Active RFID Transponders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID transponders can be classified into three types based on their power source and communication capabilities. Passive RFID transponders rely on the energy from the RFID reader to transmit data. Active RFID transponders have their power source and actively broadcast signals to the reader. Semi-active RFID transponders also have their power source but only use it to power certain functions, while still relying on the reader for communication. Each type has its advantages and limitations, making them suitable for various applications in different industries.</p></p>
