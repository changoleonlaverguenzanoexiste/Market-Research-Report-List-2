<p><strong>The Football Cleats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SG</li><li>AG</li><li>FG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Football cleats are categorized by the type of playing surface they are designed for. SG (soft ground) cleats are made for wet or muddy grass fields and have longer metal studs for added traction. AG (artificial ground) cleats are suitable for artificial turf and have shorter, rubber studs to provide grip without damaging the surface. FG (firm ground) cleats are versatile and can be used on most natural grass fields. Players should choose cleats based on the playing surface to optimize performance and minimize injury risks.</p></p>
