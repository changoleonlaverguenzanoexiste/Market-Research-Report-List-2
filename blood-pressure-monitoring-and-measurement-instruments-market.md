<p><p>Blood pressure monitoring and measurement instruments market is categorized into three main types: Sphygmomanometers, Ambulatory and Automated BP Monitors, and Blood Pressure Transducers. Sphygmomanometers are manual devices used by healthcare professionals to measure blood pressure. Ambulatory and Automated BP Monitors are portable devices that provide continuous monitoring of blood pressure over a period of time. Blood Pressure Transducers are sensors that convert blood pressure readings into electrical signals for monitoring and recording. These instruments play a crucial role in the diagnosis and management of hypertension and other cardiovascular conditions.</p></p>
