<p><p>Vanadium-based catalysts are commonly used in various industrial processes due to their high catalytic activity. Two popular types of vanadium-based catalysts include γ-alumina vanadium-based catalysts and pseudo-boehmite vanadium-based catalysts. γ-alumina vanadium-based catalysts are known for their stability and resistance to high temperatures, making them ideal for applications requiring long-term usage. On the other hand, pseudo-boehmite vanadium-based catalysts have a high surface area and porosity, leading to increased reactivity and efficiency in catalyzing chemical reactions. Both types offer unique benefits for different industrial processes.</p></p>
