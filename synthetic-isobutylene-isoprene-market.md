It is known for its high resistance to heat, ozone, and chemicals. Halogenated IIR refers to IIR that has been modified with halogens, such as chlorine or bromine, to improve its properties, such as increased heat resistance and flame retardancy. Both regular and halogenated IIR are widely used in various industries for applications requiring high durability and resistance to harsh environments.</p></p>
