<p><p>Mortar plasticisers are additives used in construction to improve the workability and performance of mortar. They are available in two main forms: powdery and liquid. Powdery mortar plasticisers come in a powdered form that is typically mixed with dry mortar ingredients. Liquid mortar plasticisers, on the other hand, are in a liquid form that can be easily mixed with water before adding to the mortar mix. Both types of mortar plasticisers help to increase the plasticity and workability of the mortar, leading to better adhesion and strength in construction projects.</p></p>
