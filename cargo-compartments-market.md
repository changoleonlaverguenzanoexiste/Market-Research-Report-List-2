<p><p>Cargo compartments are classified into four main types: Class A, Class B, Class C, and Class E markets. Class A markets consist of large cargo compartments suitable for bulk items such as grains and minerals. Class B markets are medium-sized compartments suitable for items like automotive parts and electronics. Class C markets are small compartments ideal for perishable goods like food and pharmaceuticals. Lastly, Class E markets are specialized compartments for dangerous goods such as chemicals and explosives. Each type of market caters to different types of cargo based on size and sensitivity.</p></p>
