<p><p>Direct Thermal Linerless Labels are available in various market types such as Flexo Printing, Digital Printing, and Offset Printing. Flexo Printing utilizes flexible relief plates to transfer ink onto the labels. Digital Printing involves creating labels directly from digital files, making it ideal for small runs and customization. Offset Printing uses plates to transfer ink onto rubber blankets before applying it to the labels. Each market type offers unique advantages in terms of quality, speed, and cost-effective labeling solutions for different businesses.</p></p>
