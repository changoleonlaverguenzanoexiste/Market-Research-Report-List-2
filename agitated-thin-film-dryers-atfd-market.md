<p><p>Agitated Thin Film Dryers (ATFD) are types of dryers used in various industries for drying of heat-sensitive materials. The two main market types are Vertical Agitated Thin Film Dryers and Horizontal Agitated Thin Film Dryers. Vertical dryers are best suited for applications requiring low residence time and heat-sensitive materials, while Horizontal dryers are used for high-viscosity materials and when a long residence time is required. Both types offer efficient drying with minimal heat exposure, making them popular choices in the industry.</p></p>
