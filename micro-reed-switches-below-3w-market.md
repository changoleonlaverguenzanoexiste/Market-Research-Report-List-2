<p><p>Micro reed switches below 3W are widely used in various applications, with two main types available in the market: Normally Open Type and Normally Closed Type. Normally Open Type switches are in open state when there is no magnetic field, and it closes when a magnetic field is present. On the other hand, Normally Closed Type switches are in closed state in the absence of a magnetic field and open when a magnetic field is detected. These switches offer reliable and precise performance in low power applications.</p></p>
