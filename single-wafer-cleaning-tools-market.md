<p><ul><li>Dry Process Cleaning Tools</li><li>Wet Process Cleaning Tools</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single wafer cleaning tools can be divided into two main types: dry process cleaning tools and wet process cleaning tools. Dry process cleaning tools use methods such as plasma cleaning or vapor phase cleaning to remove surface contaminants from wafers without the use of liquids. Wet process cleaning tools, on the other hand, utilize chemical solutions and water to clean wafers. Both types of tools play a crucial role in ensuring the cleanliness and integrity of wafers in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
