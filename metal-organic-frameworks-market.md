<p><p>Metal-organic frameworks (MOFs) have significant applications in gas storage, adsorption separation, and catalysis markets. In adsorption separation, MOFs can selectively capture specific molecules, making them valuable in separation processes. Additionally, MOFs can act as catalysts in various chemical reactions due to their unique structures and metal nodes, providing high catalytic activity and selectivity in a wide range of reactions.</p></p>
