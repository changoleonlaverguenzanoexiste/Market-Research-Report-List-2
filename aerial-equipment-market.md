<p><p>The aerial equipment market can be classified into three main types based on how they are powered and operated. Unpowered aerial equipment includes ladders and scaffolding that require manual labor for operation. Self-propelled aerial equipment such as scissor lifts and boom lifts are powered by electric or diesel engines and can be controlled by the operator. Vehicle-mounted aerial equipment, like cherry pickers and bucket trucks, are mounted on vehicles for easy transport and operation at height. Each type caters to different needs and applications in various industries.</p></p>
