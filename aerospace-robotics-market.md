<p><p>Aerospace robotics market is classified into different types based on their configurations and capabilities. SCARA robots have a cylindrical work envelope and are widely used for assembly applications. Articulated robots have multiple joints and offer high flexibility for a range of tasks. Cartesian robots have a rectangular work envelope and are ideal for precise positioning. Other types include delta, polar, and parallel robots, each with unique features suited for specific aerospace applications. Overall, these robots play a crucial role in enhancing efficiency and precision in aerospace manufacturing processes.</p></p>
