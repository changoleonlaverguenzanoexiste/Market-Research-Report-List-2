<p><p>Novolac resins are a type of phenolic resin commonly used in a variety of industries. They can be classified into two main market types based on their properties: thermoplastic and thermosetting. Thermoplastic novolac resins are those that can be melted and reformed multiple times, making them suitable for applications such as coatings and adhesives. In contrast, thermosetting novolac resins undergo a chemical reaction when heated and molded, becoming permanently rigid and durable, making them ideal for use in high-performance composites and engineering plastics.</p></p>
