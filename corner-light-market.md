<p><p>Corner lights are an important safety feature for vehicles, helping to increase visibility and reduce accidents. The market for corner lights can be divided into three main types: halogen/incandescent, xenon/HID, and LED. Halogen/incandescent corner lights are the traditional option, providing a warm and bright light. Xenon/HID corner lights use high-intensity discharge technology to produce a cooler and more intense light. LED corner lights are the most advanced option, offering a long lifespan, energy efficiency, and a bright, clear light. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different preferences and needs.</p></p>
