<p><p>Anion exchange resin market includes two main types: strong base anion resin and weak base anion resin. Strong base anion resin has a higher capacity for removing ions from water, making it more suitable for heavy-duty industrial applications. Weak base anion resin, on the other hand, is more selective in removing specific ions and is commonly used in residential water softening systems. Both types of resin play a crucial role in various water treatment processes, providing solutions for different levels of ion removal requirements.</p></p>
