<p><p>A twin-tube damper is a type of shock absorber commonly used in automotive suspension systems. It consists of two nested tubes - an inner working tube and an outer reserve tube. The inner tube contains the piston and valves, while the outer tube acts as a reservoir for hydraulic fluid. Twin-tube dampers are known for providing a smooth and comfortable ride by effectively absorbing and dissipating the energy generated when a vehicle encounters bumps or uneven road surfaces.</p><p>The global Twin-Tube Damper Market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years, with a projected CAGR of 9.7% during the forecast period. <p><p>Twin-tube damper market consists of two main types: hydraulic type and pneumatic type. Hydraulic twin-tube dampers work by using hydraulic fluid to absorb and dissipate the energy produced by the vehicle's suspension system. Pneumatic twin-tube dampers, on the other hand, use compressed air to achieve similar results. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring a smooth and stable ride for vehicles by controlling the movement of the suspension system and minimizing vibrations and bumps on the road.</p></p>
