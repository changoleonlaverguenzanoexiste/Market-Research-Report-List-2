<p><strong>The LCD Backlighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thin Side View</li><li>Side View</li><li>Top View</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LCD backlighting market can be categorized into three types based on the location of the LEDs within the display: Thin Side View, Side View, and Top View. Thin Side View backlighting places LEDs along the edges of the display, providing a slim profile. Side View backlighting positions LEDs behind the panel, offering a uniform light distribution. Top View backlighting places LEDs at the top of the display for efficient light output. Each type caters to different design requirements and applications in the LCD industry.</p></p>
