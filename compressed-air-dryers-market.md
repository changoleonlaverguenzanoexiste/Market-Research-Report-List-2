<p><ul><li>Regenerative Desiccant Dryers</li><li>Refrigerated Dryers</li><li>Deliquescent Dryers</li><li>Membrane Dryers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compressed air dryers are used to remove moisture from compressed air systems to prevent equipment damage and ensure optimal performance. The main types of compressed air dryers include Regenerative Desiccant Dryers, which use a desiccant material to absorb moisture; Refrigerated Dryers, which cool the air to condense and remove moisture; Deliquescent Dryers, which absorb moisture through a hygroscopic material; and Membrane Dryers, which use a selective permeable membrane to remove moisture. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different applications.</p></p>
