<p><p>Warehouse guardrails are categorized into two main types based on their weight capacity and durability - Heavy Duty and Medium Duty. On the other hand, Medium Duty guardrails are designed for lighter applications where lower impact collisions may occur. Both types offer effective protection and safety measures, with Heavy Duty guardrails being ideal for high traffic areas and Medium Duty guardrails suitable for less demanding environments.</p></p>
