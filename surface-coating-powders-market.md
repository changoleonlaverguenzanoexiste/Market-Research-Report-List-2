<p><p>Surface coating powders are used in various industrial applications such as laser cladding and thermal spray. Laser cladding involves melting a surface coating powder onto the substrate using a high-energy laser beam, creating a protective layer that enhances durability and resistance to wear and corrosion. Thermal spray involves heating the surface coating powder and propelling it onto the substrate, creating a hard, wear-resistant coating. Both processes are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for improving the performance and longevity of components.</p></p>
