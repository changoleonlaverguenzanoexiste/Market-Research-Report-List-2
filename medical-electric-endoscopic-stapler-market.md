Linear staplers are commonly used for gastrointestinal and thoracic surgeries due to their straight firing mechanism. Standard staplers are versatile and can be used in various types of surgeries, providing consistent and secure closures. Circular staplers are specifically designed for creating circular anastomoses in surgeries like colorectal and gastric procedures. Each type offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific needs of the procedure.</p></p>
