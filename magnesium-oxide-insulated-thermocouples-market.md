<p><ul><li>Grounded Type</li><li>Ungrounded Type</li><li>Exposed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnesium Oxide Insulated Thermocouples come in three main types - Grounded Type, Ungrounded Type, and Exposed Type. Grounded Type thermocouples have the two thermocouple wires welded together at the tip, providing a fast response time. Ungrounded Type thermocouples have the wires insulated from the sheath, reducing electrical interference. Exposed Type thermocouples have the wires exposed at the tip for direct contact with the measurement surface. Each type serves specific purposes based on the application requirements.</p></p>
