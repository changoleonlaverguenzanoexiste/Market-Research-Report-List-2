<p><strong>The Lighting Capacitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Type Lighting Capacitor</li><li>Oil Filled Type Capacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lighting capacitor market consists of two main types: dry type lighting capacitors and oil filled type capacitors. Dry type lighting capacitors are filled with a solid dielectric material, making them maintenance-free and suitable for indoor use. On the other hand, oil filled type capacitors contain mineral oil for better cooling and insulation properties, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Both types of capacitors are used in lighting systems to improve power factor, reduce energy consumption, and enhance overall system efficiency.</p></p>
