<p><ul><li>Flat Tappet</li><li>Roller Tappet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tappets are components found in engines that help control the opening and closing of valves. There are two main types of tappets: flat tappets and roller tappets. Flat tappets have a flat bottom surface that makes direct contact with the camshaft lobes, while roller tappets have a roller on the bottom surface that reduces friction and wear. The flat tappet market is more traditional and widely used, while the roller tappet market is growing in popularity due to its better performance and longevity.</p></p>
