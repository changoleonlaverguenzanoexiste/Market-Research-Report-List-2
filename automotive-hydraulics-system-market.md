<p><p>The Automotive Hydraulics System market consists of four major types: Brake, Clutch, Suspension, and Tappet markets. The Brake market involves the use of hydraulic pressure to assist in slowing down or stopping a vehicle. The Clutch market relies on hydraulic fluid to engage and disengage the transmission from the engine. The Suspension market utilizes hydraulic systems to absorb shocks and maintain the stability of the vehicle. The Tappet market involves hydraulic components that help regulate the opening and closing of engine valves for optimal performance.</p></p>
