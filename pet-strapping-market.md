<p><p>PET strapping is widely used for packaging and securing products in various industries. There are two main types of PET strapping available in the market: Embossed PET strapping and Smooth PET strapping. Embossed PET strapping is textured, providing better grip and friction to prevent slippage, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, Smooth PET strapping is smooth and flexible, suitable for lighter loads and general packaging needs. Both types offer strong tensile strength and durability, making them efficient solutions for securing goods during transportation.</p></p>
