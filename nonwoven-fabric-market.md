<p><strong>The Nonwoven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spun-Bonded Nonwoven Fabric</li><li>Needle-Punched Nonwoven Fabric</li><li>Spiny Nonwoven Fabric</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spun-Bonded Nonwoven Fabric is made by extruding continuous filaments onto a moving belt, creating a strong and durable material. Needle-Punched Nonwoven Fabric involves mechanically interlocking fibers through a needle-punching process, resulting in a fabric with high strength and density. Spunlace Nonwoven Fabric is created through a hydro-entanglement process, producing a soft and smooth material. Other types of nonwoven fabrics include melt-blown, thermal-bonded, and chemical-bonded, each with unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
